Interview subtext: Why should I hire you? What makes you a good fit for this role?
Interview strats: Elevator Pitch or 3 S's
3 S's: 
	 your Sucesses: Accomplishments, things that you have accomplished or succeeded at
	 your Strengths: Character Strengths, and/or Technology Strengths. What are you good at, what sets you apart
	 your Situation: What you've been doing recently, and what you're doing currently. Where do you want to be, what do you want to be doing. What do you want to work with, who, what role?
For the pitch, plan it out. Determine my successes, determine my strengths, quick example of it, and what's my situation, make it personal but professional -> Why they should hire me
